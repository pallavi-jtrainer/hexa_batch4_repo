Directives -> modifies the DOM by changing its appearance or layout.

@Directive 
	selector: to identify to a directive in the HTML template
	input: is used to provide a data to a property
	output: used to enumerate event properties of directive
	providers: used to inject a service or component into directive

Types of Directive
1. Component
2. Attribute directive
	ngClass, ngStyle
3. structural directive
	ngIf, ngFor, ngSwitch
